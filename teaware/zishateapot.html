Zisha Teapot

$200.00

Color: Light purple and brown.

Capacity: 175ml(5.92oz).

Period: 1990's.

Description

This product is made from Yixing. Yixing teapots are meant for use with black and oolong teas, as well as aged pu-erh tea. They can also be used for green or white tea, but the water must be allowed to cool to around 85 °C (185 °F) before pouring the water into the pot.

Suggested Teas:Dark oolong tea, black tea or Pu-erh tea.
