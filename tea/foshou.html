Rock Tea - Fo Shou

$30.00 – $100.00

Dry leaf: Curly and uniform strip, gentle roasty.

Liquor Color: Deep orange and clear.

Flavor: Mellow, sweet and mosit with aroma of cream.

Description

Rou Gui is a kind of oolong tea, also is one of the most famous Wuyi Rock tea. The name literally means "cassia" . The distinctive sweet aroma is highly recommended and can be brought out up to 7 steepings. Besides its best tasty, the tea also has a role of anti-aging, eliminating cold to stop pain, keeping beauty, warming spleen and stomach, and even promoting blood circulation.

Origin: Wuyi Mountains, Fujian Province.

Brewing: 5g/125ml at 100°C for 30 sec, add 10s for successive infusions. Over 10 infusions.

Storage: Sealed well in a cool, dry, dark location.
