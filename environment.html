Teashop's responsibility to environment

Here at Tea House, we strongly believe in social and environmental responsibility. We make every attenpt to protect environment through our bunisess actions.

ORGANIC TEA FARMING

As the increasing demand of tea, we switched most of tea garden to organiic tea farming in China, which would protect habitats and wildlife, keep the productivity of the land. The promotion and implementation of organic tea industry is our priorty and mission. We has focused on organic for two reasons: environment and consumers, which we consider as our very essential philosophy of the company.
Currently, Tea House teas all come from organic tea gardents in limited quantities, and are grown without chemical fertilizers or pesticides.In Tea House, everyone shares a sense of mission - protect our environment, cares our custormers as well as the employees and suppliers.

RUDUCE CARBON FOOTPRINT

The process by tea is dried and processed requiers a lot of energy. Here in Tea House, first thing we did to prevent this is to avoid using firewood in the drying process. Our main suppliers are from Wuyi Mountians in China, where there are high annual rainfall and all-season river flows. Therefore, we rely more on renewable energy such as hydropower and wind power.
We also accept loose tea, which encourages our suppliers to reuse tea to improve carbon footprint. Composting tea instead of tossing it in the trash will benefit the earth. We are committed to make environmentally conscious decisions at every step of the tea chain wherever possible. We know there is plenty of room for improvement, but we take pride knowing there are efforts in place.

RECYCLE

Our tea packaging are eco-friednly. All of them are either recylable, reusable or biodegradable, compostable. We still encourage every customer to not compost tea packages at home as it will take a pretty long time and result in tea temples strewn across the flowerbeds. We always let our customers pop them in with the food wast. Besides, we recyle at least one type of waste such as cardbaords or paper cups, which also helps to reduce our carbon footprint.
We are also offering water filtration services and bottleless coolers - we encourage customers to say NO to plastic bottles.
We make sure that all paper products, plastics and glass are recycled through the cities recycling program.
