Huangshan Mao Feng

$30.00 – $50.00

Dry leaf: Thin, flat, slightly curled,and shaped like "sparrow tongue".

Liquor Color: Green and slightly yellow.

Flavor: Taste sweet and mellow with orchid aroma.

Description

Huangshan Maofeng: One of the top ten famous tea in China, is a kind of green tea. The tea is originated from Huangshan, Anhui province, where climate is mild, the number of rainfall is abundant and the soil texture is great, so there is very suitable for the growth of tea tree. As for Huangshan Maofeng tea, the technology of picking tea-tea leaves and drying tea is very delicate, purely manual making.

Origin: Huangshan, Anhui Province.

Brewing: 3g/150ml at 90°C for 1 min. 4 – 6 infusions.

Storage: Sealed well. Keep refrigerated.
