Tie Kwan Yin

$14.00 – $60.00

Dry leaf: Flower fragrance & fresh baked goods.

Liquor Color: Clear bright yellow.

Flavor: Creamy, toasty, subtle fragrance.

Description

Top grade Tie Guan Yin Tea (also known as Iron Buddha, Buddha of Mercy, Chinese Oolong) . Exceptional buttery, flowery and sweet aroma with a lighter taste than the fall pick. A fantastic tea - our very best quality and most popular! It started subtly with a warm, comfy roastiness. Through successive infusions, tea leaves gradually open up and fragrance overflowing.

Origin: Anxi, Fujian Province.

Brewing: 3g/125ml at 100°C for 45 sec, add 10s for successive infusions. 7-10 infusions.

Storage: Sealed well in a cool, dry, dark location.
