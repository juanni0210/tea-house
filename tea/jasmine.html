Jasmine Pearl Tea

$20.00 – $46.00

Dry leaf: Flat with many tiny white furs, leaves color is in yellow and green.

Liquor Color: Clear light green.

Flavor: Pear, custard, fruit punch, Jasmine flavor.

Description

Highest grade Jasmine green tea made with real jasmine flower. Uses the tenderest and finest jasmine silver pekoe tips rolled into small balls, each ball is hand-rolled, creating a smoothness and floral aroma. A refreshing, light but distinctive smooth taste.

Origin: Hangzhou, Zhejiang Province.

Brewing: 3g/150ml at 90°C for 1 min. 4 – 6 infusions.

Storage: Sealed well. Keep refrigerated.
