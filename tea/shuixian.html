The leaves are dark in colour and are long and twisted. The leaves have been heavily roasted, this tea is very smooth and mellow with a bright golden colour and a soft.

Origin:Wuyi Mountains, Fujian Province.

Brewing: 3g/125ml at 100°C for 30 sec, add 10s for successive infusions. Over 10 infusions.

Storage: Sealed well in a cool, dry, dark location.
