Oolong Tea

As a semi-oxidized tea, oolong tea, falls between the green and black tea categories. The symbolic feature of Oolong is green leaves with a red frame. With combining the advantages of the process of green and black tea, oolong tea provides a vast range of choices of aroma as well as taste.
